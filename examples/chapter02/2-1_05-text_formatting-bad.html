Even when I'm inside a paragraph block, there are restrictions. I shouldn't use blockquotes inside the paragraph, for example.

Once upon a midnight dreary, while I pondered, weak and weary, Over many a quaint and curious volume of forgotten lore, While I nodded, nearly napping, suddenly there came a tapping, As of some one gently rapping, rapping at my chamber door. "'Tis some visitor," I muttered, "tapping at my chamber door- Only this, and nothing more."
Above I forgot to close the paragraph tag, and I didn't make the horizontal rule tag self-closing. That is a no-no.

Another no-no was using the br tags to make a paragraph break, but the validator won't catch that. It won't even object to the use of the italics tag and the bold face tag, although they are now considered bad form. It will, however, object to the underline tag.
